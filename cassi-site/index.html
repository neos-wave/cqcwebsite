Built for the transition

The framework keeps moving. Your evidence shouldn't get stranded.

As the CQC shifts toward sector-specific frameworks, Cassi re-maps your evidence underneath you. Tools hard-wired to today's framework will have to be rebuilt — Cassi was designed to adapt.

Until 2024

KLOEs & periodic inspection

Prepare hard before a scheduled visit, then relax. Evidence was a once-a-year scramble.

Now · SAF

Quality Statements, continuous

Always-on assessment. Ratings can move at any time. Evidence has to be live and defensible all year.

Late 2026 →

Sector-specific frameworks

A return to sector-specific Key Lines of Enquiry. The mapping is configurable — it updates without disrupting your homes.

Evidence you can stand behind

AI that structures your real evidence — it never invents it.

Inspectors increasingly assess how evidence was produced, not just what it says. Cassi is built for that scrutiny from the ground up.

Fully traceable

Every line of evidence links back to a real staff action, timestamp and outcome. Nothing is fabricated; everything traces to source.

Human in the loop

Cassi surfaces and structures; your managers confirm. It supports the registered manager's judgement — it never replaces it.

Your data, protected

UK-hosted, encrypted, and aligned to NHS Data Security & Protection standards. Anonymised only — and never sold.

Structures, doesn't generate

It orchestrates evidence your staff already created. This is not a policy generator — the distinction inspectors now care about.
